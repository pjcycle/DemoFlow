## [1.0.0] - 2026-05-21

### Added

- **Recording** — Full-screen recording on primary display with floating controller
- **PiP Camera** — Independent floating camera preview, always-on-top, cross-Spaces
- **Screen Drawing** — Floating toolbar + transparent canvas with 6 tools and 5 color presets
- **Video Cutting** — Drag-and-drop workflow with timeline trimming, crop, audio denoise/EQ
- **Audio Extract** — MP3 extraction from local files and online URLs
- **Dual-build support** — AppStore (no yt-dlp) and Release (with yt-dlp) configurations
- **Localization** — English and Simplified Chinese
- **Global hotkeys** — For PiP (`⌘⌥P`), Drawing (`⌘⌃S` / `⌘⌃X`)
- **macOS 14.0+** Apple Silicon support
